Hotel Marulhos

The project restores an old traditional village home turning it into a shared student house for four people. Keeping each unit fully equipped with bathroom and private areas, the common spaces such as the living room and kitchen open up to a patio articulating the heart of the house.

A strong material palette that perfectly differentiates the common areas from the private ones. Inside, a broken section chasing the original cover. Outside, the facade is simply solved and integrated into the urban environment of the village.

Location: Alfara del Patriarca, Valencia
Area: 150sqm
Year:2021
Status: Sharing moments
